Since April 2010, I'v been studying interactive design and development, and made works at IAMAS in Japan. I graduated from IAMAS in March 2012.

Before IAMAS, I studied environmental engineering at the University of Tokyo.

After graduation, I moved to Hamburg, Germany and having experience as the web developer in Hi-Res Hamburg.

My Main job in my internship is making visual, shapes, animation, design with JavaScript or openFrameworks for one project.

I'm good at making visual and interaction with programing such as C++, Action Script3.0, JavaScript, processing. I love using new technology such as Kinect and iPhone.

Personally I'm making physical engine library for animation and interaction design using JavaScript.
